### 9.1 Evolution A ‚Äî Real corresponding-adjustment double-entry ledger (analytics ladder: rung 1 ‚Üí 2)

**What.** The page is a tier-B browsing dashboard over 30 PRNG-generated agreements and 60 synthetic ITMOs, with a documented guide‚Üîcode mismatch: the promised double-entry corresponding-adjustment accounting (`Net_NDC_contribution = ITMOs_issued ‚àí ITMOs_cancelled`) does not exist ‚Äî `correspondingAdj` is a random boolean, and quality scores are draws in plausible bands, not ICVCM assessments. **How.** (1) Tables `a6_agreements`, `a6_itmo_units`, `a6_ca_entries` ‚Äî the CA entry is a paired debit/credit row; an unbalanced pair is a constraint violation, making double-claiming structurally impossible to record.
