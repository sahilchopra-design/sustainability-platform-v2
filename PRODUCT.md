# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Risk, ESG, and sustainable-finance professionals at banks, asset managers, insurers, and corporates — analysts, portfolio managers, and risk officers. They work in long, focused sessions reading dense tabular data, charts, scenario models, and regulatory disclosures. Many screens are also shown to clients and stakeholders, so the interface is both a working tool and a credibility signal.

## Product Purpose

A² Intelligence is a sustainability risk-analytics platform spanning 800+ analytical modules across ~108 domains (climate risk, carbon/emissions, sustainable finance, regulatory disclosure, physical/transition risk, sector and sovereign analytics). It exists to turn fragmented climate, ESG, and financial-regulatory data into decision-grade analysis. Success looks like an analyst trusting the numbers at a glance, finding the right module fast, and reading complex data without friction or fatigue.

## Brand Personality

Precise and institutional. Authoritative, exact, trustworthy — calm confidence over flash. Voice is expert and understated. The interface should feel like a serious instrument for professionals, in the spirit of a refined modern data tool (Linear/Stripe-grade craft) carrying an established navy-and-gold institutional identity.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS / "AI-made" aesthetics: cream/beige body backgrounds, gradient text, identical icon-card grids, the big-number hero-metric template.
- Flashy / consumer marketing energy: bright decorative gradients, playful illustration, heavy decorative motion.
- Crypto / "trading terminal" gimmickry: neon-on-black, glassmorphism, glowing status theatrics beyond tasteful data density.
- Cluttered, over-dense screens that cram information without hierarchy or breathing room.

## Design Principles

1. **The instrument disappears.** Earned familiarity over novelty — standard affordances, consistent component vocabulary screen to screen, so attention stays on the data.
2. **Trust through precision.** Tabular numerals, exact alignment, verified contrast, deliberate spacing. Sloppiness reads as unreliable analysis.
3. **Density with breathing room.** Show what professionals need, but give it hierarchy and rhythm; legibility is never traded for cramming.
4. **One identity, modernized.** Keep the navy/gold institutional anchor; raise the craft (real type, cooler neutrals, refined states and motion) rather than restyling for novelty.
5. **Calm confidence.** Restraint is the brand. Accent and motion convey state and importance, never decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥4.5:1, large text ≥3:1, verified against real backgrounds (no muted gray body text on tinted near-white). Visible keyboard focus states on every interactive control. Color never the sole carrier of meaning (pair with icon/label/text). Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` with crossfade/instant fallbacks for every animation.
